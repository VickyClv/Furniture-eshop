Who we are

VIOL was founded by Vicky and Olga in the town of Athens, Greece. Vicky and Olga are two college students of Athens University of Economics & Business who major in computer science. What brought them together was their common interest in programming and home decor. That's why they decided to creat VIOL. However what makes VIOL different from other furniture companies? In VIOL our main focus is the quality of our products. We want our industry to become greener with more sustainable products, yet without sacrificing style over quality. Our goal in the near future is to reclaim and upcycle furniture.

Our Purpose

By creating this website we want to provide eco-friendly and wallet-friendly products. The majority of our furniture and furniture packages are consisted of recycled products. In every product we have added a section called 'environmental information' that empasizes on what makes each product eco-friendly. Furthermore we have concluded many different style-groups with unique designs.

Our Vision

Balance style with sustainability.
Limit the use of materials harmful to our health.
To promote an eco-friendly lifestyle and reduce our carbon footprint.
Create more alternative approaches
Reclaim and upcycle furniture
