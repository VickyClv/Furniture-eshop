What is good to know

Additional information

For 6 to 8 people. Recommended for indoor use only.

What's included

1 extension sheet included.

Environmental information

By utilizing a renewable material such as wood in this product, we avoid using fossil or non-renewable resources.

By using particle board with a layer of solid wood on the surface instead of solid wood all over, we use less wood per product. Thus, we exploit the raw materials in a better way.

Advantages

The table has no visible joints when you use it closed, thanks to its clever design. You can easily adjust the length to make room for all kinds of activities like reading, drawing and even board games with the kids. The legs of the table stay in the corners when you extend it, so you have enough room for the chairs. One person can quickly and easily extend the table before the guests arrive. The extra sheet can be stored under the table top for easy access. Each table is unique, with natural variations in the shades of the wood. It is easily combined with chairs and benches of the same series or why not with other VIOL chairs.
