What is good to know

For 4 to 6 people. Handle with care! A worn edge or scratch can cause the glass to crack and/or break. Avoid knocks from the side, where the glass is more sensitive. For added security, retighten the screws when necessary. Recommended for indoor use only.

Advantages

An extension leaf is included. The glass surface of the table lets light through, making the table discreet and harmonizing with your space, even if it is limited. A safety glass table top, not easily stained and easy to clean. The table can be easily extended by a single person to accommodate 4-6 people, depending on your needs.
