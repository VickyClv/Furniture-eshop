Environmental information

Table surface

By using particle board with a layer of solid wood on the surface instead of solid wood all over, we use less wood per product. Thus, we exploit the raw materials in a better way.

We want to have a good impact on the planet. That's why we want by 2030, all materials used in our products to be recycled or renewable and sourced responsibly.

Advantages

The table is very stable thanks to the metal frame. The melamine surface is stain and scratch resistant and easy to clean. An ideal choice for families with children. The table is available in two sizes, for 2-4 people. With its simple, understated design, it easily matches many styles. You can create a larger table by placing two or more MELLTORP tables next to each other. You can for example place a small MELLTORP table next to a larger one.
