What is good to know

Additional information

For 6 people. The tabletop features a 3mm layer of solid oak. Recommended for indoor use only.

Environmental information

By utilizing a renewable material such as wood in this product, we avoid using fossil or non-renewable resources.

We want to have a good impact on the planet. That's why we want by 2030, all materials used in our products to be recycled or renewable and sourced responsibly.

Advantages

The table features a monastic design with an authentic wood feel. The monastic style is emphasized by the design on the edges. Oak is an extremely durable wood with strong "waters". Its color darkens and becomes more beautiful over time, acquiring a golden-brown tone. Each table is unique, with natural variations in the shades of the wood. You are making a good choice for the environment, as with the chipboard construction method covered by a solid wood surface, less wood is used for its production.
