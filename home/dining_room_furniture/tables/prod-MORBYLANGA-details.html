What is good to know

Additional information

The tabletop features a 3mm layer of solid oak. Recommended for indoor use only. For 6 people.

Environmental information

By utilizing a renewable material such as wood in this product, we avoid using fossil or non-renewable resources.

By using particle board with a layer of solid wood on the surface instead of solid wood all over, we use less wood per product. Thus, we exploit the raw materials in a better way.

Advantages

The table features a monastic design with an authentic wood feel. The monastic style is emphasized by the design on the edges. Oak is an extremely durable wood with strong "waters". Its color darkens and becomes more beautiful over time, acquiring a golden-brown tone. Each table is unique, with natural variations in the shades of the wood.
