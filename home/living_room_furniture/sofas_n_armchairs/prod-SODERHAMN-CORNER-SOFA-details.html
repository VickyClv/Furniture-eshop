Product Details

What is good to know

Security and compliance

The abrasion resistance of the cover has been tested with 50,000 abrasion cycles. From 15,000 cycles and above means that the cover is suitable for everyday use at home, while over 30,000 cycles means great abrasion resistance. The cover has a lightfastness level of 5 (fading resistance ability) on a scale of 1 to 8. According to industry standards, a lightfastness level of 4 or higher is suitable for home use.

Advantages

he SÖDERHAMN seating range allows you to sit deep, low and soft, providing back cushions for extra support. Lightweight design with high legs and slim lines but particularly comfortable thanks to the stretchy weave on the bottom and the highly elastic foam on the seat cushions. The sections of the sofa can be used individually but also combined in different ways to create the size and shape that suits you. And if at some point you need a larger sofa, you can always add additional units to the already existing composition. Back cushions are removable so you get support where you need it. For example, place several pillows behind your back for a more upright posture, or use them to be more comfortable when lying down and reading. The depth of the sofa is ideal for relaxing or for a nice nap. You can choose whether to use the sofa with or without arms, depending on the effect you want. The high legs allow you to clean the floor under the sofa. The cover is made of Tonerud, a soft felt-like polyester fabric with a two-tone effect. The cover is easy to keep clean as it is machine washable and can be easily removed and reattached. 10-year warranty. See more about the conditions in the guarantee leaflet.
